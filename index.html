About Our Clinic

At Atamandike Private Clinic, our mission is to provide compassionate and comprehensive medical care to every patient. We believe in treating the whole person - not just symptoms - with dignity and respect in a welcoming environment.

"We are committed to delivering exceptional healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and excellence, ensuring the well-being of our patients through the hills and valleys of life."

Founded in 2010, we have been serving the community of Mwalaoyera and surrounding areas for over a decade. Our clinic was established with the vision of making quality healthcare accessible to everyone in our region, regardless of their background or financial situation.

Our team of dedicated healthcare professionals works tirelessly to ensure that each patient receives personalized care tailored to their specific needs. We combine medical expertise with genuine compassion to create a healing environment where patients feel valued and understood.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every patient with empathy, kindness, and understanding through life's challenges.
  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards of medical care and professionalism.
  • Integrity: We practice honesty and transparency in all our interactions.
  • Accessibility: We strive to make quality healthcare available to everyone in our hillside community.
  • Community Focus: We are dedicated to serving the unique needs of the Mwalaoyera region.

Our Medical Services

At Atamandike Private Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of medical services to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our community in the hills of Ntcheu. Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to providing high-quality care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

General Consultation

Comprehensive health assessments, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of medical conditions. Our experienced physicians provide personalized care plans for all ages.

Family Planning

Counseling and services including birth control options, fertility awareness, preconception care, and reproductive health education.

Emergency Care

Immediate medical attention for acute illnesses, injuries, and urgent health concerns. Our emergency team is available 24/7 for critical situations.

Post Abortion Care

Compassionate medical care, counseling, and support following pregnancy loss. We provide both physical and emotional care during difficult times.

STI Services

Testing, treatment, and counseling for sexually transmitted infections with complete confidentiality and sensitivity to patient concerns.

Under 5 Services

Specialized pediatric care including vaccinations, growth monitoring, nutrition counseling, and treatment of childhood illnesses.

Minor Surgeries

Procedures such as wound suturing, abscess drainage, mole removal, and biopsies performed in our sterile procedure room.
